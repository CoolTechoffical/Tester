Kerala Education Context

Kerala has strong public education participation with district-level coordination. Holiday decisions may vary based on weather alerts, local events, and administrative orders.

How this site helps

  • Shows color-different category tags (rain, fixed, strike, school strike).
  • Displays district and sub-district level updates in easy cards and tables.

  • Provides backend-ready structure for admin login and live updates.
